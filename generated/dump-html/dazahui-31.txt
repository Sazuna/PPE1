<p>然而外界说明故事放入太多元素，「一下子跑出女巫」、「一下子跑出连环杀人犯李秀妍」，再过不久现在「医院女医生突然又领便当了？」不仅如此，在接下来的剧情当中，又即将演到「触手怪物攻击人类。」种种大杂烩加在一起，网友都纷纷表示「实在是看不懂到底在演甚么？」明明演员的演技都过硬，「却毁在编剧手中。」<br><a data-fancybox="images" data-caption="黑洞" href="https://a.ksd-i.com/a/2021-05-31/135785-908369.jpg"><img src="https://a.ksd-i.com/s/480x_86400_d0b9a3584dd63aaefbd357bfbfff5831/static.koreastardaily.com/2021-05-31/135785-908369.jpg"></a><span class="caption">黑洞</span><br><a data-fancybox="images" data-caption="黑洞" href="https://a.ksd-i.com/a/2021-05-31/135785-908366.jpg"><img src="https://a.ksd-i.com/s/480x_86400_51a20824c54576b9a9cc0cb3d097a269/static.koreastardaily.com/2021-05-31/135785-908366.jpg"></a><span class="caption">黑洞</span></p>
<p>此外，女主角金玉彬与男主角李浚赫，分别在学校和医院带领众人逃难，在这过程当中几乎没有交集，也遭受到许多网友批评。该剧由《他人即地狱》编剧郑怡道作家剧执笔，全剧总共12集，即将於本周五、六播出最后结局。<br><a data-fancybox="images" data-caption="黑洞" href="https://a.ksd-i.com/a/2021-05-31/135785-908364.jpg"><img src="https://a.ksd-i.com/s/480x_86400_6bb30fd573b3d82ce69eb1be5a056525/static.koreastardaily.com/2021-05-31/135785-908364.jpg"></a><span class="caption">黑洞</span><br><a data-fancybox="images" data-caption="黑洞" href="https://a.ksd-i.com/a/2021-05-31/135785-908367.jpg"><img src="https://a.ksd-i.com/s/480x_86400_254143969818db73ff148c187221d69b/static.koreastardaily.com/2021-05-31/135785-908367.jpg"></a><span class="caption">黑洞</span></p>
